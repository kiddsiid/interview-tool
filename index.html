How to run a Reactioner interview
  1. Pick the participant slot below (and optionally type their real name).
  2. For each slide, read the items aloud in order — problem, quote, promise, feature, price.
  3. After each one, ask their honest gut reaction (❤️ love / 👍 like / 👎 dislike) and tap the matching button. The notes box is private to you.
  4. Move to the next slide once every reaction is captured.
  5. When done, open the 🔥 Heatmap tab to see what landed across all participants, or 🔗 Share with participant to send the slides to someone to react on their own phone.

Heads-up: You opened this file directly from your hard drive (a file:// URL). The "generate link" option below won't work for someone on a different device — they can't reach a file on your computer. Use "Download standalone participant file" instead — it produces a small HTML file you can email or text. Or, for the easiest path of all, use the same-device option.

📱 Option 1 · Send to their phone (best for mobile interviews)

Generates a link with the slides embedded and opens your device's share sheet so you can text, WhatsApp, iMessage, or email it straight to the participant. They tap the link on their phone, react, and send the reactions back the same way.

🤝 Option 2 · Same device (in person)

You hand them your laptop, tablet, or phone. Reactions auto-sync to the selected slot — when they hand it back, the heatmap is already filled in.

Use your browser's Back button when they're done to flip back to your interviewer view.

Advanced · Standalone HTML file (for offline / no hosting)

Generates a tiny self-contained HTML file you can email as an attachment. The participant opens it locally on their phone or computer — no internet needed. Useful if you're sharing this with someone who can't open links.

📥 Import reactions from a participant
